About Me

Professional Background

I am a dedicated neuro rehabilitation specialist with extensive training in chiropractic care and orthopedic manual therapy. My practice focuses on evidence-based treatments to help patients recover from neurological conditions, sports injuries, and musculoskeletal disorders.
